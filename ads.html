Ad Strategy Overview

COSMO runs a 3-stage funnel: cold traffic to free quiz, warm retargeting to $9 report, buyer retargeting to $47 membership. All ads drive to the quiz page (index.html) as the entry point — the quiz converts better than a direct sales page because it's interactive and free.

Stage 1: Cold

Broad audiences. Astrology interest, MBTI interest, self-improvement. Hook with curiosity or identity. CTA: free quiz.

Stage 2: Warm

Website visitors who didn't purchase. Lookalikes of buyers. CTA: $9 report, direct link to results/OTO.

Stage 3: Retarget

People who started quiz but didn't complete. Email list. CTA: finish your blueprint.

Creative Format

Video (Reels/TikTok) for cold. Static image for warm. Carousel for comparison/education.

Targeting

Interests: Astrology, Human Design, MBTI, personality tests, self-help, manifestation, spirituality.

Budget Split

60% cold video, 25% warm static, 15% retarget. Scale what converts at <$3 CPL on cold.
